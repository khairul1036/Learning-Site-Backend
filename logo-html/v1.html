Logo Design Rationale

This logo combines a 3D cube with a network grid to represent the "3D Cluster" concept. The red cube symbolizes the three-dimensional aspect and technology, while the intersecting black lines form a cluster network representing connectivity and learning. The Orbitron font provides a futuristic, tech-oriented feel, and the minimal tagline reinforces the institute's mission. The design is fully scalable for print and digital applications.
